Industries We Serve

Experience Across Industrial Environments

IH-FoA works across the sectors where occupational exposure hazards are most present, bringing consistent methodology and clear reporting to each engagement.

Manufacturing

Primary expertise: metal fabrication, plastics, automotive, food processing, chemical manufacturing.

Construction

Silica, lead, asbestos, and noise exposure assessment for general and specialty contractors.

Healthcare

IAQ, chemical exposure, and workplace safety programs for hospitals and clinical facilities.

Warehousing

Forklift exhaust, combustible dust, and ergonomic exposure assessments for distribution centers.

Schools

IAQ investigations, mold assessments, and occupant health complaint investigations for K-12 and higher education.

Commercial

Office buildings and property management: IAQ, ventilation assessments, and Legionella risk.
