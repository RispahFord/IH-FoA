FAQ

Common Questions

What types of air sampling do you perform?+
We sample for silica, metals (lead, cadmium, chromium), VOCs, combustible dust, and a wide range of airborne chemical contaminants. All sampling uses NIOSH/OSHA certified methods with AIHA-LAP accredited laboratory analysis and full LOD/LOQ documentation.
How quickly can you deploy onsite?+
In most cases we can be onsite within 24–48 hours. Contact us directly for urgent needs or potential regulatory situations.
Do you help write compliance programs after identifying gaps?+
Yes. We don't just hand you a findings list; we develop practical, usable written programs (respiratory protection, HazCom, hearing conservation, lockout/tagout, and more) based on your specific facility and processes.
Are your sampling reports OSHA-defensible?+
All reports include complete chain-of-custody documentation, NIOSH/OSHA sampling method citations, full analytical results with LOD/LOQ, and are designed to withstand regulatory scrutiny.
What industries do you serve?+
Manufacturing, warehousing, construction, healthcare, schools, commercial office facilities, and property management companies across 12 Southeast states.
